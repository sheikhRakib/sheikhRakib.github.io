Weblog is a web-based blog site project based on Laravel Framework (v8) where anyone can read blogs without authentication, though writing articles requires membership. There are three types of members here, normal users (can manage articles of their own), moderators (responsible for maintaining the quality of contents), and admins(can give access to others and many more).

kitcat

c/c++ || glut

KitCat is a C/C++ and GLUT based game project where players feed fish to two cats named Kitty and Catrina. Other than the regular fish, there are deadly and special fish as well. Special fishes have more points than regular ones. Players' sole focus is to feed the cats, avoid deadly fishes, and gain a maximum score.
